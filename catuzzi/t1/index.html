About Us


Catuzzi Consulting provides clients with credible, viable management analysis and focused solutions to business projects.
Deliveries are based on operational excellence.

As a Firm, we carry out an independent and fact-based survey of the client's Performance.
We rely on facts as they remove ambiguity, provide understanding and align People.
Objectivity is the all-encompassing management language.
As a Firm we work with reliable data to provide credible supported proposals.
We team up as partner with our clients to develop and implement recommendations
and we passionately work with leaders who have the qualifications and the resourcefulness
to execute them.
